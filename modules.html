Level 1

Communicating what we want


Asking our partner for what we want is often difficult. We don’t know the words to communicate, we might feel shy about asking for what we want, we may be afraid of hurting our partner’s feelings, or we simply may not now what we like. This module is about practicing giving each other touch and beginning to open up communication.

Level 1

Touching


Here is a video showing different ways that you can touch. You can use a firm touch of your whole hand, you can do kneading, you can use light touch of your finger tips, you can try using your finger nails, you can make small circles with one finger.

Practise touch communication


It can be difficult to put into words what you want your partner to do. Some suggestions of feedback to give might include: harder, softer, I really loved what you were doing a second ago, could you do that again? A great method to do this is the praise sandwich method. Tell them something you appreciate, tell them what you would like to be different, affirm that you like what they’re doing now.
